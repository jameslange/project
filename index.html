About Me

I'm from the United States. Growing up in small town America as the son of a farmer and a schoolteacher, it has been my dream to travel. Following this dream, I earned a Bachelor of Science in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Purdue University. While working in the restaurant industry I saved money in order to travel to South America, during which I learned Spanish. This experience helped me to start a career in the tourism industry where I worked for three years as an intranational tour guide, leading Europeans on multi-week tours in the National Parks and cities across the US.

My father is a farmer, my father's father was a farmer, his father was, you guessed it, a farmer.
